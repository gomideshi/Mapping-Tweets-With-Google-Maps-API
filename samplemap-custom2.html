The Artists of #ACL

For both musicians and fans, Austin City Limits is an event of profound importance.

In addition to boasting a massive stage, the event gives people the opportunity to share their passions. People come to ACL to learn about new artists, rediscover acts from years ago, and of course, indulge in their current favourites. All the while, they are communicating about these experiences on twitter.


Mapping Data

The map to the left is an effort towards realizes this information.

Within the map, tweets containing the terms 'ACL' coupled with any musical artist have been plotted to indicate where this activity is taking place.

This representation clearly contains tweets related to a wide variety of musical artists (Lorde, Eminem, etc.) -- but what's different isn't as important as what's the same: all of this communication comes from people talking about their experiences related to the artists' presence at the event.

Sometimes, it seems, that the nature and direction of communication isn't so telling as the volume.
